Usage

Clicking on a bar of either of the yearly graphs will update the visualization on the left, so for the extraction data the chosen year will be displayed in months and clicking on a year of the stacked histogram will display the locations of the corresponding earthquakes on the map of the Netherlands. Clicking on one of the blocks of the legend of the stacked histogram will display all earthquakes in the chosen magnitude range of the currently selected year.
